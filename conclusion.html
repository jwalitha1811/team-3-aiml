CONCLUSION

The survey conducted in our area to assess the level of satisfaction with the municipal corporation and its services revealed a mixed but insightful response from the residents. Out of the total respondents, 32 individuals expressed satisfaction with the work carried out by the municipal corporation, indicating that a portion of the population is content with the services provided. On the other hand, 18 individuals reported dissatisfaction, highlighting areas where the municipal corporationâ€™s performance may require attention or improvement. The feedback gathered points to a range of factors influencing public opinion. While many residents appreciate certain aspects such as waste management ,road maintenance, and healthcare facilities, others have expressed concerns over sanitation, water supply, and infrastructure maintenance. This divide suggests that while the municipal corporation has had some positive impact, there are still significant areas where residents feel their needs are unmet.
