ABSTRACT

On Municipal corporation survey was conducted to assess the accessibility and utilization of municipal services in Ranganayakula peta, Nellore District. The primary objective was to determine the extent to which residents benefit from essential services provided by the Municipal Corporation, including water supply, sanitation, waste management, and public infrastructure. Data was collected from various households and individuals to analyse the reach and efficiency of these services. The findings highlight the percentage of residents utilizing municipal services and the primary service providers catering to their needs. This study also emphasizes the importance of municipal services in improving the quality of life and suggests measures to enhance accessibility and awareness among underserved communities.
